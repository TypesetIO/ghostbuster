Myeloid-derived cells producing IL-6 (in the mesenteric lymph nodes) and both IL-6 and IL-1b (in the spleen) are responsible for Breg cell induction in arthritis (Rosser et al., 2014b) , whereas IL-21-producing CD4 + T cells located in the spleen are responsible for Breg cell induction in EAE (Yoshizaki et al., 2012) .
