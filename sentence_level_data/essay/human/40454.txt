Over the past decade, the quantity and quality of social relationships in later life have become one of the main challenges facing an aging society (Valtorta, Moore, Barron, Stow, & Hanratty, 2018) .Globally, up to 50% of older people are at risk of social isolation and about one third of those aged 60 years and over experience loneliness in later life (Landeiro, Barrows, Musson, Gray, & Leal, 2017) .
