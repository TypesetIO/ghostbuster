Youth who reported physically being threatened or injured had significantly higher odds of rating their physical health as poor or fair (OR: 2.1, 95% CI: 1.7-2.7),their mental health as poor or fair (OR: 2.1, 95% CI: 1.1-4.4),forgone physical health services (OR: 3.0, 95% CI: 2.3-3.8),forgone mental health services (OR: 2.5, 95% CI: 1.8-3.3),self-harm (OR: 3.6, 95% CI: 2.7-4.8),suicide ideation (OR: 3.1, 95% CI: 2.3-4.2), and suicide attempt(s) (OR: 4.3, 95% CI: 3.2-5.8).
