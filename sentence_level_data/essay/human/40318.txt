Influencers can be classified in line with the content they generate (Bloggers, YouTubers, Podcasters, Social Posts Only), the impact level they bring (Celebrities, Key Opinion Leaders), the niche they operate (nano-influencers -experts in a very specific field), the number of followers they have (micro-influencers (10000 -400000), macro-influencers (400000 -1 million), mega-influencers (more than 1 million followers in one social media service), nano-influencers (might be less than 1000) [35] .
