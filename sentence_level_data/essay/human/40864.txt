Perceived health-promotion importance was evaluated by asking participants to rate the importance (1 = not important, 2 = somewhat important, 3 = very important) of 13 health-promoting self-care activities (regular exercise, use of vitamins, prayer or faith, close friends, avoiding stress, luck or fate, inherited characteristics, diet and nutrition, positive thinking, avoiding germs, avoiding smoking, avoiding alcohol, using car seat belts, and getting routine medical checkups).
