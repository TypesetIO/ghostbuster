In contrast to the complex composition of other gutassociated communities, the honeybee gut microbiota consists of only eight major bacterial species (based on 16S rRNA gene sequence similarity .97%): two alphaproteobacterial and two gammaproteobacterial species, two members of the phylum Firmicutes, one betaproteobacterium and one species of the genus Bifidobacterium (Moran et al., 2012; Sabree et al., 2012) .
