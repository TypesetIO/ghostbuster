Alterations in the gut microbiota composition have been reported at the onset of various gastrointestinal disorders like intestinal bowel syndrome [12] , ulcerative colitis (UC) [13] , etc., as well as in other organ disorders like autism [14] , multiple sclerosis [15] , asthma [16] , cystic fibrosis [17] , urogenital disorders [18] , skin disorders [19] , etc.
