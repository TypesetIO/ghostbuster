PLS-SEM has been widely applied in the following fields;marketing (e.g., Henseler et al., 2009 ), management information system (e.g., Chin et al., 2003) , organization (e.g., Sosik et al., 2009) , behavioural sciences (e.g., Bass et al., 2003) , and business strategy (e.g., Hulland, 1999) .SEM can be decomposed into two sub-models, which are measurement model and structural model.
