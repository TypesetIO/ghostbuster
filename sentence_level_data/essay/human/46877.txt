Enhanced responses to food predictive cues in the midbrain and prefrontal cortex after the HF/HS dietary intervention is in keeping with a sizable preclinical literature showing that the consumption of energy-dense palatable food rich in fat and sugar rewires this reward circuitry to enhance incentive motivation, 19, 26, 30, 66, 67 an effect that can even transfer to the offspring of dams fed an HFD.
