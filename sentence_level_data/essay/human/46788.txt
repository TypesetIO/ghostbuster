The nomenclature for this deeply branching lineage originated from Orbus hercynius CN3 T , the first species characterized from the order Orbales (Volkmann et al., 2010) .Recently, a second species of the genus Orbus has been described; Orbus sasakiae C7 T was isolated from the gut of the butterfly Sasakia charonda (Kim et al., 2013) .Sequencing-based methods revealed the existence of additional taxa within the family Orbaceae.
