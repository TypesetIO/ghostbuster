Cyber bullying is "an aggressive, intentional act carried out by a group or individual, using electronic forms of contact, repeatedly and over time against a victim who cannot easily defend him or herself" (Smith, Mandavi, Carvalho, & Tippett, 2005, p. 6) .Victims and perpetrators of cyber bullying suffer from series of psychological problems such as frustration, sadness (Mishna, Mclukie & Saini, 2009) , depression, confusion, guilt, shame, self harm, distress (Hinduja & Patchin, 2007; Ybarra, 2004) .
