The forests of the KWLS are mainly composed of Northern Tropical Dry Deciduous forests (5B) and subgroup 6B -DS1-Zizyphus scrub, DS1-Dry deciduous scrub, and SS4 -Dry Grasslands (Champion and Seth 1968) according to the vegetation map prepared by the French Institute, Pondicherry."Dhonk" (Anogeissus pendula) is the dominant tree species in the sanctuary constituting nearly 80% of the vegetation cover.
