Several studies have demonstrated a role for the dorsal (DH) and ventral (VH) hippocampus (Czerniawski, Yoon, & Otto, 2009; Esclassan, Coutureau, Di Scala, & Marchand, 2009b; McEchron, Bouwmeester, Tseng, Weiss, & Disterhoft, 1998; Quinn, Oommen, Morrison, & Fanselow, 2002; Yoon & Otto, 2007) , and extra-hippocampal areas (Bang & Brown, 2009; Esclassan, Coutureau, Di Scala, & Marchand, 2009a; Kholodar-Smith, Boguszewski, & Brown, 2008) in the acquisition of trace fear conditioning.
