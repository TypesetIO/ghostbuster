The CO 2 emissions decreased by 18.7% (182 Mt CO 2 ) compared to the first quarter in 2019, including decreases of 12.2% (92 Mt CO 2 ) in the industrial sector, 61.9% (62 Mt CO 2 ) in transport, and 23.9% (28 Mt CO 2 ) in construction (Wang et al., 2020) .Some data showed that there was a reduction of nearly 1 million tons of carbon emissions during the lockdown, which is equivalent to 6% of global emissions.
