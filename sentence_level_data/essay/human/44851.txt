Within the time frame of March 2020 through October 2020 the emergency departments had a 24% increase in mental health concerns for children ranging in age from 5 to 11 (Abrams, 2022) .According to The Grief in School Survey conducted in 2020, 95% of all educators agreed with the statement that social and emotional support for students has never been more needed (Hart Research Association, 2020) .According to the Gallup parent polling, three in ten school-aged students are suffering emotionally (Calderon, 2020) .While the statistics support the need for social and emotional instruction and training for educators, the current practices have yet to align with what is needed to support growth in students' social and emotional functioning.
