Although there is much discussion on the external validity of economic experiments like these (List and Shogren 2006) , in particular those that deal with moral and public issues, their shortcomings seem to be more related to magnitudes (size of the effects detected) than qualitative insights (nature of the relationships observed) (Levitt and List 2007) , and their results, on the whole present reasonably solid degree of validity (Chang et al. 2009 ).
